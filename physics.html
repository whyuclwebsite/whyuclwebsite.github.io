About the Course

If you ask someone why they're studying Physics, they'd always tell you the same thing: "it's the key to understanding the universe," "I want to know how the world works," "I love Maths," "Stephen Hawking is my spirit animal" or something along those lines. If you're reading this, you probably think the same. And there's nothing wrong with that. Studying Physics at UCL has been an exhiliarting yet challenging journey.

Modules: What modules can you do in first year?

In the first term, you can switch between the three corse Physics degrees, you can do either Physics, Astrophysics or Theoretical Physics.

Your core modules for pure physics will include:

  • Second Year
  • Third Year

Since I do pure Physics, the modules in first year I had to complete were:

  • Atoms, Stars and the Univsere
  • Classical Mechanics
  • Mathematical Methods I
  • Mathematical Methods II
  • Thermal Physics
  • Waves, Optics & Acoustics
  • Practical Physics
  • Developing Effective Communications
